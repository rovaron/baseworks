## Cross-Phase Integration Gaps

### GAP-1 — Email invite template not localized (warning)
- **Affects:** I18N-01, I18N-02, INVT-02
- **Phases:** 08 → 09
- **File:** `packages/modules/billing/src/templates/team-invite.tsx` (note: template lives in `billing` module — misplacement worth flagging)
- **Detail:** Template hardcodes `"You're invited to {organizationName}"`, `"Accept Invitation"`, etc. `sendInvitationEmail` callback in `auth.ts` passes no locale. Pt-BR recipients get English emails.

### GAP-2 — SkipToContent label hardcoded (tech-debt)
- **Affects:** A11Y-03, I18N-02
- **Phases:** 07 → 08
- **File:** `packages/ui/src/components/skip-link.tsx:13`
- **Detail:** `"Skip to content"` is a literal string. No `label` prop. Pt-BR users see English skip link.

### GAP-3 — InviteDialog errors not announced to screen readers (warning)
- **Affects:** A11Y-04, A11Y-05, INVT-01
- **Phases:** 07 → 09
- **File:** `apps/web/components/invite-dialog.tsx:180-186`
