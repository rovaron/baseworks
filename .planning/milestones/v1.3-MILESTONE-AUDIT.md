## Recommendations

The milestone is incomplete by design — phases 21–23 are not yet authored. Two paths forward:

1. **Plan and execute phases 21–23** to close the 11-requirement gap. This is the ROADMAP's intent. Recommended sequence:
   - Phase 21 first (DOC-01 docker-compose unblocks Phase 18/20 human-UAT items by giving operators a real Tempo + Sentry stack).
